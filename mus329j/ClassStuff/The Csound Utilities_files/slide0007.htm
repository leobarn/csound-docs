“Direct” versus “Fast” Convolution
lDirect convolution is extremely expensive, computationally: on the order of N2 multiplications (N is length of longest signal)
lUse Csound dconv opcode for this (Moss)
l“Fast” convolution takes advantage of the fact that convolution in the time domain is equivalent to multiplication in the frequency domain.
lFFTs are performed on both input signals, then spectra are multiplied, then IFT applied to result.
