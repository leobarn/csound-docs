convolve and –U cvanal
lThe Csound convolve opcode implements “fast convolution”
lFirst use cvanal on one signal, usually the shortest, called the Impulse Response.
lOutput file is an FFT of the IR, typically (but not necessarily) named convolve.nnn
lThe convolve opcode internally performs an FFT on the other signal, then multiplies their spectra, then performs an Inverse Fourier Transform on the product
