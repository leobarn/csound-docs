The Phase Vocoder
lDeveloped by Portnoff (‘76); introduced by J. A. Moorer (‘78);  implemented by Mark Dolson (’83) lSimilar to STFT technique, but provides a method of accurately tracking the deviation from the center frequency of each analysis bin lLike FFT, PV can be viewed as a bank of band-pass filters. The more filters used, the finer the frequency resolution, but the slower the execution, and the coarser the temporal resolution.
