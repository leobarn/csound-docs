Short Time Fourier Transform
lFor some applications, it is useful to take multiple FFTs of an audio signal at some time interval
lThese are sometimes referred to as STFTs, or Short Time Fourier Transforms
lThe individual FFTs are computed from a “windowed” portion of the audio signal
lThe length (number of samples or “points”) in the analysis window determines the number of frequency “bins” in the output (only ½ are used)
