Syntax of pvoc
lktimpnt – time pointer into analysis file (in seconds)
lkfmod – pitch modification factor (1 = no change)
lifilcod – pvoc.#  or “filename”
lispecwp (optional) – if non-zero, attempts to preserve the spectral envelope while its frequency content is varied by kfmod. The default value is zero
l
ar pvoc ktimpnt, kfmod, ifilcod[, ispecwp]
