Spooky Halloween Noise Study

Due October 31st, 2006

Create an original composition of 2-3 minutes duration consisting solely of filtered noise. You may use any of the Csound random units to generate the noise, and any of the filter units we have discussed in class (tone, reson, butterworth, and pareq filters). Create your composition exclusively in Csound, please. You may also use any other Csound opcodes we have covered, but remember that the main purpose of this assignment (other than scaring the little kids in your neighborhood on Halloween night) is to learn what you can do with noise and filters. As usual in this course, I will place much more importance on technical implementation, efficient programming, good coding style, etc., than I will on creativity. But a little imagination never hurts!
