17. Neural Processing using Csound

Pedro A. G. Batista

Everybody is looking for the perfect sounds. Most of the times, for the common of mortals, this cant be easily achieved. Instead we end up with a collection of not-so-great sounds, each one bearing its small resemblance to the masterpiece. Wouldnt it be nice, to be able to squeeze the "soul" out of those attempts, and combine them to create that ultimate sound that is eluding us?

Well, somehow, I have this weird notion, that this can be tried with the aid of neural networks and distributed processing. And I believe that Csound can be used to implement some neural algorithms, at least for experimental purposes.

Wanna bet? :)
